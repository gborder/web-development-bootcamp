The Best Movies According to Gus

My top 3 movies of all-time.

  1. Interstellar (2014)

    When Earth becomes uninhabitable in the future, a farmer and ex-NASA pilot, Joseph Cooper,
    is tasked to pilot a spacecraft, along with a team of researchers, to find a new planet for humans.

  2. Matrix trilogy (1999 - 2003)

    Thomas Anderson, a computer programmer, is led to fight an underground war against powerful
    computers who have constructed his entire reality with a system called the Matrix.

  3. Snatch! (2000)

    A number of criminals consisting of arms-dealer, thieves and gangsters attempt to get hold of
    an elegant diamond which is stolen.
